Intimacy Tracker (做了么 / ぬいた？)

Overview

Intimacy Tracker ("the App") is a personal life-tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data you enter into the App — including records, partner names, materials, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not use cookies or web beacons
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not have user accounts or registration
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or files

Location Data

The App may request location permission to auto-fill a location field when creating a record. This data is processed entirely on-device via a one-time reverse geocoding request to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim API. The resulting city/region name is stored locally on your device only. We do not store or track your location history on any server.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We use RevenueCat as a purchase management service, which may process anonymous transaction data. No personal content from your records is shared with these services.

Data Export & Import

The App provides export/import functionality for backup purposes. Exported data is saved as a JSON file on your device. You are solely responsible for the security of exported files.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security measures. The App provides an optional PIN lock feature for additional privacy. We recommend:

  • Enabling the App's built-in PIN lock
  • Using your device's screen lock feature
  • Being cautious when exporting data

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
